# CRIMEA IS UKRAINE 🇺🇦

This intelligent assistant is designed for quick information search based on the materials of the ["Crimea is Ukraine"](https://crimea-is-ukraine.org/en/) website.

You can ask the assistant any question — it will analyze the website's articles, archive documents, and scientific research, and then provide an accurate answer with links to the respective primary sources.
